# Welcome to the Panelin Wiki

**Panelin** is an intelligent AI agent designed to act as a **BMC Assistant Pro**. It specializes in quoting, sales evaluation, and training for construction materials like Isopanels (EPS/PIR), Drywall, and Waterproofing.

*   **Sales Evaluation**: Analyzes sales interactions to improve team performance.
*   **Training System**: Uses historical data to train sales representatives.
*   **Source of Truth**: Relies on a strict hierarchy of JSON files for pricing and specifications, ensuring accuracy.
